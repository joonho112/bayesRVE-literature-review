Appendix B — Literature database & pillar × theme map

This appendix folds the navigable literature database into the book. Every row is a paper in the read corpus — the 138 entries the review was built from — each with a clickable DOI, its pillar and theme, and the claim-IDs (CL-Pn-NN) it serves. A pillar × theme coverage matrix and a short how this review was built reading map follow.

How to read the table. Rows are grouped by primary pillar (P1–P4; P0 foundational claims appear in the Claim-IDs served column, not as a group). Citekey is the stable @citekey used throughout the book and bibliography; Reference is the short author–year–title–venue form; DOI links to https://doi.org/…; Claim-IDs served lists the catalogued claims the paper supports or qualifies; Tier (A/B/C) records the reading-priority tier. All 138 papers are owned and read (each has a critique note).

B.6 How this review was built (reading map)

The review was assembled through a disciplined, documented methodology:

  1. Framing. The positioning thesis (the four pillars P1–P4), the 37-claim framework (CL-Pn-NN, the join hub), and the 27 overclaim guardrails (G-01…G-27) were established before discovery began, defining the evidentiary scope and discipline.
  2. Discovery. The existing literature was indexed and the search extended across the four pillars, with the two gap claims adversarially audited; a literature database and a pillar × theme map were built (this appendix’s ancestors).
  3. Corpus. The reused corpus was staged, the acquired gap papers added, and the bibliography finalized to a 138-paper registry.
  4. Reading. All 138 papers were read into structured critique notes, each tagged to the claim-IDs it serves, with a coverage and caveat-conflict review.
  5. Synthesis. The claim-to-evidence map, the four pillar briefs (P1–P4), the consolidated gap-and-contribution narrative, and the annotated outline were built and externally reviewed.
  6. Authoring. The synthesis was written into this seven-chapter book, every load-bearing sentence anchored to a catalogued claim and an exact bibliographic citation, held to the guardrails and the locked gap wordings.

Every @citekey in the chapters resolves to a bibliography entry with a DOI (citation integrity verified); the two GAP-ASSERTIONs (CL-P2-02, CL-P4-06) rest on documented systematic absence across this catalogued corpus, not on any single row of the table above.
